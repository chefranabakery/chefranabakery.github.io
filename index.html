Rana Bakery

Situated at the left bank of the Beas, Rana Bakery is serving Manali since 1993. Devastating flood in 1995 could not shred the zeal of the founder Mr. B.S.Rana to serve the public. Rana bakery is a journey toward the continuation and enhancement of quality and freshness of the products such as macaroons, biscuits, buns, fan, doughnuts, burgers, patties, cakes and pastries famous among locals and tourists. We love to add values and be a part of people's happiness. Smiling and contented faces impel us to serve the quality we are ment for.
